Our Story

Brew & Beans began as a simple idea: create a space where neighbors could gather over great coffee and pastries. We source beans from small, sustainable farms and roast in small batches to preserve flavor and aroma. Our baristas are passionate about coffee, community, and making your day a little brighter.
